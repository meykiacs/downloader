    Sales Rental Service

    Whatever kind of process you are aiming for, Logisticon Water Treatment offers a solution to enable you to achieve your water treatment goals.

    Logisticon can help you with the treatment of drinking and process water as well as the purification of municipal and industrial wastewater. You can choose from a (semi) permanent, mobile or pilot solution – on either a purchase or rental basis – or you can opt for outsourcing if you are looking for a full-service solution.

    Logisticon designs and builds its treatment systems in-house at its own centralised production facility giving you direct access to technical staff who will work alongside you to ensure that the process technological design, engineering and construction are fully realized and meet your requirements.

    About us

    Logisticon Water Treatment

    Logisticon designs and builds complete water purification installations in its own factory for the preparation of drinking water and process water but also for the purification of municipal and industrial wastewater. In addition, in the field of environmental engineering Logisticon focuses on groundwater purification and remediation. Logisticon's installations are available on a for sale or rental basis.
